Why Donate?

Education in India is primarily provided by public schools (controlled and funded by the government at three levels: central, state and local) and private schools. Under various articles of the Indian Constitution, free and compulsory education is provided as a fundamental right to children aged 6 to 14. The approximate ratio of public schools to private schools in India is 7:5. This is not sufficient comparing to the population of India. With your help we can achieve the primary aim of free education for all.

Why us?

We, as responsible citizens have started this donation camp to support the government in providing free education to kids between 6 to 14. We will ensure that every bit you donate will go to the fundings directly by providing every detail about the donation you made was used for.
